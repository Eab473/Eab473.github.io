Recommended/Planned Changes

Based on the feedback that I have collected, I plan on making the following changes:

Improving Design

  • Images: I plan to add some graphics to make the webpage look better. I think theres are some aras that need to be filled to make it catch the viewers eyes and want them to come back.
  • Navigation: I need to look into why on an ipad or even a cell phone that the footer appears in the body and not the bottom of the page. I will be working on this based on the information I received.

Enriching Content

  • Parents: I will be creating a table to include some links to some useful sites for parents. I'll be adding some pictures as well.
  • Students: I will be creating a table to include links for students to access. I will be adding more graphics for them so its more appealing to them.

  • About Me: I will be creating this page to include a picture of me and possiblity my classroom.
